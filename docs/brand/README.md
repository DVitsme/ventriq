## The brand system (from the chat, verbatim decisions)

- **Name:** Ventriq — pronounced **"Ven-TREEK"** (not "Ven-trick") — Justin's own note.
- **Concept:** *Ventriq = venture + corridor.* One idea everywhere: **two lines converging to a vanishing point** — reads as a **V**, a **corridor**, and a **threshold** ("a room worth getting into"). "An architect's line, not an accelerator's rocket."
- **Logo system:** **Wordmark = primary logo** (most trademarkable). **Monogram = detachable partner** for small/square spaces (favicon, avatar, embroidery) — a solid V with a **notch cut to the vanishing point**; the negative space is the ownable part ("protect and exaggerate, not soften"). **Lockup** (mark + name) for formal use. A separate abstract symbol was considered and dropped.
- **Black-and-white first, color second** — the master logo must survive one-color (fax, embroidery, etch, photo overlay).
- **Type:** Space Grotesk **SemiBold** (OFL licensed — free for all use). Chat guidance: fine to ship now; when budget allows, have a designer **redraw the letterforms bespoke (especially the q)**. The monogram is original geometry — ownable as-is.
- **Hierarchy note:** Ventriq is the **parent brand above "Founders After Hours"** — "slightly more elevated in feel." *(✅ Confirmed 7/8: "Founders After Hours" — plural — is the official name.)*
- **Brand references from his brief:** Harlem Capital / Essence — "the warm, culturally-rooted lane."

## Palette (three tiers — see `palette.md` / `ventriq-tokens.css`)

| Tier | Color | Hex | Job |
|---|---|---|---|
| Foundation (~90%) | **Midnight** | `#101B2D` | Dominant brand color — "what you're wrapped in" |
| Foundation | **Cream** | `#F1ECDF` | Light background |
| Foundation | **Ink** | `#0B0F16` | Text & one-color logos |
| **Signature** | **Gold** | `#C9A24C` | "If someone remembers one color, it's this" — the q's tail, highlights |
| Accent (5–10%, pick ONE) | Burnt orange | `#C15A2C` | Committed provisionally in the files |
| Accent (alternate) | Electric teal | `#22B0A0` | One-line swap if Justin ratifies teal instead |

**⚠️ Open decision:** the accent is **not ratified** — the chat committed to orange provisionally; on the kickoff call Justin liked both. Get his final call. Chat caveat worth honoring: hexes are "a strong starting direction, not gospel" — the *structure* (navy+cream foundation, gold signature, one accent) is the contract.
