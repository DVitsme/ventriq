# Ventriq Voice

Justin Shaw's voice in one line: **a warm host with a teacher's structure and a pastor's cadence** — greets like family, organizes in threes, sells transformation over transaction, closes with a low-pressure invitation.

Full system: `findings/09-voice-and-tone-guide.md` (phrase bank, samples) · person/motivations: `findings/08-justin-deep-dive-person.md`. This file is the working compression.

## Registers — pick per surface

| Register | Sound | Use on |
|---|---|---|
| **Practitioner-Teacher** (~70%) | Plain, direct, receipts ("80–90% of my discovery calls"), numbered steps, defines jargon inline | Program pages, Summit, FAQ, how-it-works, emails |
| **Preacher-Poet** (~25%) | Short declaratives in sequence ("Trust first. Transactions follow."), soil/roots/forge/corridor metaphors, legacy language | Heroes, mission, About, Donate |
| **Institutional** (~5%) | Applications, committee review, acceptance letters, cohorts | Mastermind mechanics, grants |

Never: the 2017 luxury-exclusivity register ("debonair," "if you are to par") — that's the retired suit brand.

## His devices (use these; they're authentically his, not AI tells)

1. **Triads**, often rhyming: "Collaboration, acceleration, escalation" · "Capital, programming, council" · "Train the founder. Fund the vision. Forge the future."
2. **Contrast couplets**: "Not just education, but implementation" · "Not vanity, but transformation" — *his most-used move; deploy deliberately, ~once per page section, not per paragraph.*
3. **Numbered frameworks** for any process.
4. **Pain-first openers** — name the founder's frustration before Ventriq's answer.
5. **Gateway/access metaphors**: "capital stops being the reason you stall and starts being the reason you scale."
6. **Forge/refinement**: "iron sharpens iron," diamond-cut-by-diamond, "forge you into a new founder."
7. **Humility prefixes** on achievements: "I have the privilege of…"
8. **Question CTAs + invitations**: "Ready to build differently?" · "No pressure — just an honest conversation." · "If you're serious, you belong here."

## Vocabulary

**Free:** founder · purpose · council · clarity · confidence · implementation · systems · serious · real · honest · legacy · equip · forge · access · intentional · stewardship · season
**Care:** *covenant* (belongs to the suit brand) · *hustle/grind* (he's anti-burnout) · *underrepresented* (default to "all founders, with a focus on…" pending counsel — see competition/ventriq/nonprofit-founder-orgs.md)
**Never:** crush-it/beast-mode hype · get-rich language · guru/ninja · scarcity timers ("only 3 spots!") · unexplained jargon

## Faith calibration (hard rule)

**Explicit in interviews, implied in copy — sermonize structure, not scripture.** Ceiling on-site: "the life you're praying for," "calling," "stewardship," "covering." Nothing overt without Justin's explicit sign-off. His scripture moves are riffs, not citations ("write the plan and make it plain").

## Ship checklist

1. Read it aloud — would Justin say this sentence on camera? Fix anything he'd stumble on.
2. One human beat per section (a plain aside, a "P.S.", a specific number/name) — never uniform polish.
3. Couplets/triads: deliberate, not compulsive (AI-tell threshold is frequency, not existence).
4. CTAs are invitations, never pressure.
5. Em-dashes ≤1 per paragraph; vary sentence length hard.
6. Also run the `human-copy` skill checks if installed.
