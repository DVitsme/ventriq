# UTM registry — the only links we share

Rule: **never hand-type UTMs.** Share `/go/*` short links; the site stamps the
parameters. Luma stores `utm_source` on every registration (Insights → Top
Sources) and GA4 groups them via the custom channel group.

## Justin's share sheet

| Where it's posted | Link to use |
|---|---|
| Instagram bio | `ventriq.io/go/ig` |
| IG post/story about the summit | `ventriq.io/go/summit?s=ig-story` |
| LinkedIn posts | `ventriq.io/go/summit?s=li` |
| HBCU partners (per school) | `ventriq.io/go/summit?s=hbcu-howard` (swap school) |
| A speaker's audience (per speaker) | `ventriq.io/go/summit?s=speaker-royster` (swap name) |
| Speaking gigs / QR on slides | `ventriq.io/go/summit?s=speak` |
| Newsletter | `ventriq.io/go/summit?s=email` |

Vocabulary (lowercase, hyphens): `utm_source` ∈ ig-bio · ig-story · linkedin ·
speaker-〈name〉 · hbcu-〈school〉 · speaking · newsletter · ventriq-site ·
`utm_medium` ∈ social · email · partner · qr · referral ·
`utm_campaign` = forge-the-future-2026.

Keep Luma's "How did you hear about the summit?" options aligned with this
list (Instagram / LinkedIn / My school · HBCU / A speaker / Heard Justin speak /
Newsletter / A friend / Other) so self-report and UTMs pivot together.
