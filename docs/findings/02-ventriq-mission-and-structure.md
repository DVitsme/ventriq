## Nonprofit + for-profit structure

Justin explicitly sees **two entities**, mirroring the OpenAI model: [transcript]

- Mastermind, Summit, Founders After Hours, grants, pitch competitions, programming.

**② A for-profit arm (associated)**
- **Consulting, advisory, 1:1 support**, and **VC** — investing in companies directly and "becoming a partner in those ventures."
- Possibly a **for-profit software product** (the gamified founder simulator — see `03-programs-and-roadmap.md`). Justin left the software's home (nonprofit vs. for-profit) as a **"gray area."**
