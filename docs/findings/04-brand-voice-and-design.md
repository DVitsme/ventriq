## The decided identity system

**Concept:** *Ventriq = venture + corridor.* One ownable idea — **two lines converging to a vanishing point** — reading as a **V**, a **corridor**, and a **threshold** ("a room worth getting into"). "An architect's line, not an accelerator's rocket. No lightbulbs, arrows, or handshakes."

**Logo system:**
- **Wordmark = primary logo** ("Ventriq," one flat color; gold corridor tail on the q) — most trademarkable.
- **Monogram = detachable partner** for small/square use — a solid V with a **notch cut to the vanishing point**; the negative space is the ownable element ("protect and exaggerate, not soften").
- **Lockup** (mark + name) for formal use. A separate abstract symbol was considered and **dropped**.
- **Black-and-white first, color second** — the master logo survives one color on any background.

**Typography:** **Space Grotesk SemiBold** (OFL). Ship as-is now; commission a bespoke redraw of the letterforms (especially the q) later. The monogram is original geometry — ownable as-is.

**Palette (three tiers, not six equal colors):**

| Tier | Color | Hex | Job |
|---|---|---|---|
| Foundation (~90%) | Midnight | `#101B2D` | Dominant — "what you're wrapped in" |
| Foundation | Cream | `#F1ECDF` | Light backgrounds |
| Foundation | Ink | `#0B0F16` | Text, one-color logos |
| **Signature** | **Gold** | `#C9A24C` | "What people recognize you by" — the q tail, key highlights |
| Accent (5–10%, ONE) | Burnt orange | `#C15A2C` | Provisionally committed (warm/culturally-rooted) |
| Accent alternate | Electric teal | `#22B0A0` | One-line swap if ratified instead (forward/tech) |

**⚠️ Open: the accent is unratified.** The chat committed to orange provisionally; on the call Justin loved midnight and liked both orange and teal. Get his final call — one accent, not both ("a brand that uses two accents reads as undecided").
