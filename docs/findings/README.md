## What this is

An analysis of the July 8, 2026 kickoff call with **Justin Shaw** (transcript: `../transcript/7-8-26-init.md`), cross-referenced with deep independent research on Justin, his brands (**IAMJS**, **Shaw's Covenant**), his voice across seven years of interviews and social posts, and his professional background. The goal: understand Justin and his business deeply enough to build the right website — in his voice — fast.

## Executive summary

- **Who:** Justin Y. Shaw (~31, Baltimore/Owings Mills) — **Director of Programming & Partnership at the Maryland Innovation Center** (the "MIC"), founder of **IAMJS** (founder education, est. Oct 2025) and **Shaw's Covenant** (bespoke suits, 2017 — **paused Oct 2025**), Goldman Sachs 10KSB alum, co-author of *Emerge*, man of faith, husband & father. Story in one line: the overlooked 6'5" Baltimore kid who learned image + faith open doors, and has spent a decade scaling that thesis from suits → founders → ecosystems.
- **What:** **Ventriq** (V-E-N-T-R-I-Q) — a brand-new **nonprofit** supporting small-business owners, tech startups, and nonprofits — especially **underrepresented founders** — with **Capital, Programming, Council**. It's the "foundation" he's said he tithes 10% of revenue toward, and the vehicle for his stated calling to "shift global economies." Zero public footprint: **this website is Ventriq's birth certificate.**
- **The MVP driving the site:** ① **Mastermind** cohort 2 (Sept 2026; landing page needed now) · ② **The Ventriq Summit** (virtual, Aug 10–21) · ③ **Founders After Hours** community (Skool + city chapters), launched via Summit CTAs.
- **Website scope:** marketing site; third parties carry the load — **Luma (PWYW registration) + Skool (paid community, TBC) as of meeting 2 Jul 10; Eventbrite out, donations OFF until filings** (`meetings/07-10-2026-meeting-2-outcomes.md`). One brand: "Ventriq presents…"
- **Key dates (2026):** content-ready **Jul 20** → soft launch **~Aug 1** → Summit **Aug 10**.
