## The brief

Build a **Ventriq marketing site** that:
1. **Launches the brand** (brand-new nonprofit, zero prior footprint) under one unified identity.
2. **Drives Summit registrations** (→ Eventbrite) and **community joins** (→ Skool / Founders After Hours).
3. Hosts a **Mastermind landing / application page** (cohort 2, September).
4. Enables **nonprofit donations** (→ Stripe).

**Content-ready by Jul 20 · live by Aug 1 · Summit Aug 10.**
